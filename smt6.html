Test

We conducted multiple usability tests throughout our low- mid- and high-fidelity prototypes. We also conducted A/B tests to confirm which designs were more user-friendly and delightful.

From our usability tests, we were able to better layout the site to match user expectations. We also found that users were confused by the donation language that was taken from the original site. Finally, a footer that would peek out, then hide again, depending on hover state, was causing problems with users. Though it was functioning properly, a few users attributed the behavior to the site being “broken” so it was replaced with a static footer.
