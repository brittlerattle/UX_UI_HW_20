Before we could begin wireframing, the information gathered in the Define stage was used to develop user flows for the most integral parts of the site: Getting Involved and Trails. These site modules were certain to be complicated, with circular paths that intersect and branch, so it was important to carefully work through this step.

To properly include and parse all the existing information in the current site, the team utilized card sorting and site-mapping, then iterated it to better serve the users. The navbar links were distilled from 5 categories to 3 and the redundant links were removed to keep everything streamlined.

A style guide was formed that incorporated the feel of the original website, with a focus on AA Accessibility and contemporary design aesthetics. The logo was redesigned to better fit the new look and feel.
