MadPlanet Shopping App

Ideate

The main revelation coming at the beginning of our Ideate Phase was that we could solve usersâ€™ apprehension towards subscriptions and memberships by using a co-op model. MADPlanet could provide consumers resources to enhance their shopping experiences, while simultaneously supporting companies that value ethics. Instead of subscription fees going to pad the pockets of tech-bros, Co-op dues would be pooled to help ethical producers stay competitive and keep the cost of carbon neutral shipping as low as possible.

The co-op idea was tested in in-person interviews, and warmly received.

Feature Prioritization

We learned the best use of team resources was to focus on delightfullness, streamlined User Flows and ways to be totally transparent with users at every step.

Style Tile

Keeping delightfullness and ease of use in mind, the team used large buttons and bright, contemporary colors. The user needs for playfulness and simplicity were carefully balanced to not make the app feel unserious, or toy-like.
